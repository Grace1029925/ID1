From a young age I have always had a gift for putting different visuals together to tell a story. The reason for that is I am a very visual person and usually in every project I do I have always gone above and beyond on the visual aspect of it. In my opinion, visuals help relay what a person is thinking or feeling. Graphic design is the perfect for me because there are so many different directions that one can take with it.
